2. Visualizations

2.5. Use visualizations

When the visualizations is visible, SRec shows a panel with the name of the algorithm and the parameters values.

SRec allows to navigate by the animation forward and backwards step by step. The animation can be reinitializated and finished quickly, the corresponding button must be pressed.

It's possible to animate automatically the navigation with the animation buttons. The visualization will go step by step forward or backwards each several seconds (the number can be configurated by a text field or an slider bar).

SRec can realize a skip in the visualization. A jump skips all the subcalls of the current call. During the visualization, user can modify the amount of information SRec shows selecting methods and parameters he wants to watch.

For closing the visualization, the close button must be pressed.
